torchio.transforms.augmentation.intensity.random_bias_field module¶

The bias field is modelled as a linear combination of polynomial basis functions, as in

K. Van Leemput et al., 1999 Automated model-based tissue classification of MR images of the brain

It was included in NiftyNet by Carole Sudre and used in:

C. Sudre et al., 2017 Longitudinal segmentation of age-related white matter hyperintensities
